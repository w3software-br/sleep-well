About

Have you ever noticed that your thinking becomes clearer when you hear water running down a stream? Or the sensation of enlarged senses when you concentrate on the sound of the wind shaking your tree leaves? According to scientists, the sounds of nature have an effect “Restorer” for our mind. And the reason is simple: they alter the connections made in the brain, reducing our natural instinct to be alert to possible threats.

The purpose of this App is to help you relax through the sounds of nature. Whether to meditate, study or sleep. Enjoy!
